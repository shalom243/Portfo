My Journey

At first, coding was merely a puzzle I solved to earn a pat on the back from my parents, a way to showcase my intellect and gather praise. I dabbled in simple projects, crafting basic programs that performed basic tasks, all the while yearning for the approval and admiration that came with each completed project. However, as I went deeper into the realm of coding, something remarkable happened. The lines of code I meticulously pieced together transformed from mere symbols into gateways to creativity, problem-solving, and innovation, I actually understood what was happening in front of me! Each project became an opportunity to create something unique, to build digital worlds from scratch, and to leave a lasting mark on the virtual landscape.

Where I am now

As a passionate and driven teenager currently in grade 10 at Gonzaga High School, I find myself standing at the threshold of an exciting chapter in my life. Eager to explore the vast realm of technology, I have been granted a remarkable opportunity to work as an intern at TechNL, a renowned company at the forefront of innovation. This invaluable experience will allow me to further develop my skills, collaborate with industry professionals, and immerse myself in the dynamic world of technology. With each passing day, I embrace the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead, ready to push boundaries, expand my knowledge, and unlock the boundless potential that awaits me..
